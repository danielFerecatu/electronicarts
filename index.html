About EA™:

Electronic Arts™ is an American company that manufactures and markets video games for computers and game consoles. Most popular games from Electronic Arts portofolio are as FIFA Soccer sports simulators, with Need for Speed, Medal of Honor, The Sims, Battlefield and games appeared later as Burnout and Command & Conquer series. Founded in 1982 by Trip Hawkins, the company was a pioneer in the industry and the first computer games was notable promote its designers and programmers.

At first she was a company producing games at home. In the late '80 company started out home games and consoles in the early "90. EA hired later successful programs. In early 2000, it became one of the largest distributors of games. In 2007 EA ranked 8 in the ranking of the largest software manufacturers in the world. In May 2008, the company reported annual revenue of 4.02 billion dollars in fiscal year 2008. In 2010 it ranked in the top two producers of video games, topped by the Japanese company Nintendo.

The company is present in Romania since 2005, with mobile games division of EA Mobile.
